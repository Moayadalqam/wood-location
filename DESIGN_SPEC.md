## 4. Interactions & Animations

### Page Load Sequence
1. Loader appears (1.5s)
2. Loader fades out
3. Hero badge fades in (0.2s delay)
4. Hero title line 1 (0.4s delay)
5. Hero title line 2 (0.6s delay)
6. Subtitle (0.8s delay)
7. Buttons (1.0s delay)
8. Scroll indicator (1.2s delay)

### Scroll Animations
- Elements reveal with `translateY(40px) â†’ 0` + `opacity: 0 â†’ 1`
- Staggered delay for multiple items
- Trigger at 10% visibility

### Hover Effects
- Buttons: `translateY(-2px)` + shadow
- Gallery images: `scale(1.05)`
- Cards: `translateY(-10px)` + shadow
- Links: Underline expands left to right

### Navigation
- Scroll past 50px: Background blur + shadow
- Mobile menu: Slide in from right
- Active section: Link underline
